# Rasino Drugs Pvt. Ltd. - Company Website PRD

## Original Problem Statement
Build a company website for Rasino Drugs Pvt. Ltd. - a B2B pharmaceutical chemicals company that manufactures APIs (Active Pharmaceutical Ingredients), Intermediates & Fine Chemicals. Exports to 40+ countries with GMP/ISO certifications.

## User Requirements
- Product catalog with search/filter (no pricing/ecommerce)
- Product detail pages with inquiry button
- Inquiry form for bulk orders
- Certifications/Documents download section
- Full website: Home, About, Products, Services, Certifications, Export Markets, Contact

## Target Audience
- Pharmaceutical companies (B2B)
- Chemical suppliers
- Formulation houses
- Contract development organizations
- Global B2B buyers

## Core Requirements (Static)
1. Professional corporate pharma design
2. Product catalog with category filtering
3. Inquiry system for B2B leads
4. Certifications display with downloads
5. Global export markets showcase
6. Contact form functionality
